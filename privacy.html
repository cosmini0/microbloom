Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

MicroBloom collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Flashcard Content: Text, images, and other content you create or import in your flashcards
  • Learning Progress: Review statistics, difficulty ratings, and spaced repetition scheduling data
  • Google Drive Data: If you enable Google Drive sync, we access only the flashcard backup files you authorize

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display flashcards and track your learning progress
  • Optimize spaced repetition scheduling for better learning retention
  • Sync your flashcard decks across devices (if you enable Google Drive backup)
  • Generate flashcards using AI when you request this feature

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally in your browser by default
  • Google Drive sync is entirely optional and requires your explicit consent
  • We do not store your personal data on our servers
  • AI flashcard generation processes your content temporarily and does not store it

4. Third-Party Services

MicroBloom integrates with:

  • Google Drive API: For optional cloud backup (with your permission)
  • OpenAI API: For AI flashcard generation (content is not stored)
  • ExtensionPay: For payment processing of premium features

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We only share data as necessary to provide the services you've requested (e.g., Google Drive sync).

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the extension
  • Disable Google Drive sync at any time
  • Export your flashcard decks as backup files

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

privacy@microbloom.app
